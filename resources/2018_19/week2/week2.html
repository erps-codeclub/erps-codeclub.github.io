And finally...

What to do when you're done:

  1. Give your project a name.
  2. Save your project!
  3. Click on share to share your project.
  4. Click on your username in the top-right of the screen, and select 'My Stuff' from the list.
  5. Find your project, and in the 'add to' list, select this week's class.
  6. Click on your username again, and this time select 'My Class'.
  7. Have a look at everyone else's projects, and give an encouraging comment!
